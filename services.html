Event Planning

As your planner, we will make your vision standout to make your event exceptional. We prepare timelines, budgets, resources, arrange suppliers, provide emotional support, style the event, and are there with you every moment of the event.

Our services include

  • Consultations to enhance the style of your Event
  • Research, recommend best in class suppliers
  • Coordinating with suppliers on your behalf
  • Event day management with on site assistance

Catering

We offer extensive choices of dishes and cuisines to satiate a wide range of taste palettes. We specialize in Pakistani, Indian, and Continental food and our High Tea is an innovative fusion of cuisines across continents. We only use the best ingredients, a perfect balance of spices, and are happy to customize food to match your taste buds. Every food service is unique and will leave you wanting to explore further into our sumptuous menu.

We offer two ways to entertain your guests. For effortless luxury, just tell us what dishes you like and we will provide a per head menu for your event. But if you prefer to order in bulk our trays of food are easy to order and serve.

Buffet Table Setup

Why just stop at mind boggling tasty food. Our buffet table setup will transform your food presentation into a work of art. Exceptional food when matched with exceptional display will make your guests overwhelmed with joy. Our food stylists have an eye for details, every event is meticulously designed and elegantly rendered to uplifts the mood of the event.

Dessert Table Display

Our dessert table displays are irresistible for the young and the old, for the sober and the playful, for the cautious and the carefree. The colorful artwork attracts everyone and makes the desserts star of the show. May it be Cupcakes, Tiramisu, Firni in clay dishes, or Tiered Cakes, our dessert magician can infuse a sense of utter joy in satisfying the sweet tooth. Dessert table display can be ordered by itself or as part of the overall catering package.

Table Scaping

Let us make your event perfect by creating an exuberant ambiance. Our table scapes are unique and themed to your preferences. We artistically place centerpieces, tableware, candles, flowers and personalized props to build the mood of your event. These beautiful sitting arrangements will delight your guests and provide just the right impetus for heartfelt conversations.

Backdrops

Bring your event to life with our energizing backdrops. We create custom backdrops for Birthdays, Graduation, Ameen, Baby Shower, Bridal Shower and all other occasions. Pick from three backdrop types: Balloons, Flowers or Drapes and let us do the magic.

Wedding Stages

Stages are the center of attention at any event. Our elegant and functional stage designs make them shine in the wedding. Our service includes backdrop design, sitting arrangements, floral displays, mood lighting and more, which will make your wedding truly memorable. Photos on our artistically created stages will immortalize your rarest moments in life.
